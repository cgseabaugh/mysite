Graphics & Web Designer

My name is Gabe Seabaugh and I'd like to design your website.

I was raised in Placerville, California and moved to Lake Havasu City, Arizona at the beginning of 2020 with my wife and, at that time, our only daughter. I initially got into coding and web design in order to work on passion projects, but I've since decided to try my hand at it professionally.

I'm a navy veteran of a five-year enlistment during which I served as a Hospital Corpsman 8404. I spent three years in Okinawa, Japan and participated in various excercises and deployments in the South Pacific, but ultimately decided not to reenlist.

In addition to web design, I have a passion for creative writing and video game design. I enjoy storytelling. My dog's name is Henry. I like old books and shot glasses. And now I really like to design websites.
